Our Industry Expertise

KARMA Worldwide Logistics LLP provides end-to-end supply chain expertise across Automotive, Retail & E-Commerce, Industrial Engineering, FMCG, and Electronics sectors — delivering fast, reliable, and scalable logistics solutions tailored for high-volume, time-sensitive operations.

Automotive

Efficient transport of auto parts, components, and vehicles with Just-in-Time delivery and line feeding to assembly units.

Pharmaceuticals & Healthcare

Cold chain logistics for medicines, vaccines, and medical devices under strict temperature and regulatory compliance.

Retail & E-Commerce

Warehousing, packaging, and doorstep delivery for retail and e-commerce brands with real-time tracking and fulfilment.

Manufacturing & Industrial Goods

Reliable transport for raw materials and finished goods supporting manufacturing operations globally.

Relocation / Household

Comprehensive packing, moving, and delivery services for homes, offices, and businesses nationwide.

Industrial Machinery & Engineering

Heavy-lift logistics for machinery, equipment, and large-scale industrial projects with route planning and permits.

FMCG (Fast-Moving Consumer Goods)

High-volume, time-sensitive logistics for packaged food, beverages, and consumer products with timely delivery.

Electronics & Technology

Secure transportation for high-value electronics, semiconductors, and consumer tech components worldwide.
