About KARMA Worldwide Logistics LLP

KARMA Worldwide Logistics LLP, is a trusted Indian logistics company providing comprehensive solutions in freight forwarding, supply chain management, warehousing, and project logistics. Backed by years of expertise and a commitment to innovation, we ensure every shipment is handled with precision, reliability, and care.Operating exclusively across India, we specialize in Full Truck Load (FTL) transportation—delivering dependable and timely services on North-to-South and South-to-North routes. With our dedicated team, advanced systems, and strong operational network, KARMA Worldwide Logistics LLP keeps India’s supply chains moving smoothly and efficiently.
